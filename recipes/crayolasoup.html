Crayola Soup

Description

Crayola soup is really quite a horrendous dish. If you have found yourself here, you should probably just avoid this recipe. It's literally just melted crayons. There is no nutritional value here. That's all.

Ingredients

  • Crayons

Steps

  1. Seriously? Fine.
  2. Melt crayons in a pot
  3. Pour the obnoxious mess into a bowl or, as I recommend, directly into the trash
  4. Possibly ask yourself "Why did I do this? Am I really even deserving of love?
