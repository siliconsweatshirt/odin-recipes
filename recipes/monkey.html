Monkey

Description

Monkeys are intrepid, good-natured folk known for their willingness to lend a helping hand to those in need. Despite their exuberant attire and mannerisms, most monkeys are rural-raised country bumpkins who don't know doodly-squat. These creatures are just so loveable and monkey-like that you can't help but squeeze their little cheeks and pat their fuzzy lil' heads.

Perhaps the most well-known monkey amongst average people is the 45th President of the United States, Donald Trump.

Ingredients

  • Eight pounds of monkey sauce
  • Eight liters of monkey juice
  • 30 pounds of monkey matter
  • A handful of monkey material
  • A respectable set of monkey manners
  • A sprinkle of monkey magic :)

Steps

  1. Add monkey sauce, monkey juice, and monkey matter to a pot
  2. Add heat until boiling
  3. Remove from heat and let sit until nightfall
  4. Return to the concoction and add the rest of the ingredients, taking special care with the monkey magic
  5. Let sit until morning
  6. Wake up and be pleasantly surprised by breakfast in bed, prepared by the loveable monkey now standing before you (his name is Waymond)
